Frequently Asked Questions


We specialize in writing a variety of speeches including eulogies, wedding vows, bride's or groom's speeches, father or mother of the bride or groom speech, maid of honour, best-man, anniversaries, milestones, corporate speeches, graduation speeches, and more. Whether it's a personal or professional event, we can craft the perfect speech to meet your needs.

For eulogies, we understand the urgency and guarantee delivery within 24 hours. For all other types of speeches, our standard delivery time is 5 working days from the date of your order.

Your completed speech will be delivered to the email address you provide on the order form. Please ensure the email address is correct to avoid any delays.

Yes, we offer revision meetings to ensure your speech meets your expectations. For eulogies, you are entitled to one revision meeting. For all other speeches, you can have up to two revision meetings if necessary.

After you receive your draft speech, you can schedule a meeting to discuss any changes or amendments. For eulogies, you can schedule one meeting. For other types of speeches, you can schedule up to two meetings. These meetings can be conducted via phone, video call, or email, depending on your preference.

If you require an expedited service for non-eulogy speeches, please contact us directly to discuss your needs. We will do our best to accommodate your request, though additional fees may apply.

You can place an order through our website by filling out the order form. Provide as much detail as possible about the event, audience, and any specific points you want included in your speech.

We require details about the event, the audience, and any specific messages or themes you want included. For personalized speeches, providing anecdotes, personal stories, and key points can help us craft a more tailored and impactful speech.

All our speeches are written from scratch, tailored specifically to your needs and the event. We do not use templates or pre-written speeches, ensuring a unique and personalized speech every time.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not satisfied with the final speech, please let us know during your revision meeting(s) so we can make the necessary changes. Our goal is to ensure you are completely happy with your speech.

Payment can be made securely through our website using a credit card or debit card. Payment is required at the time of placing your order.

In addition to speech writing, we also offer consultation services to help you prepare for delivering your speech, including tips on public speaking and presentation skills. Please contact us for more information.

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All information you provide is kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of writing your speech. We do not share your information with third parties.
